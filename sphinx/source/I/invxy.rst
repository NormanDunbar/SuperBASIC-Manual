INVXY
=====

+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Syntax   |  INVXY x%, y%                                                     |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Location |  HCO                                                              |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+

This is a simple command which draws a crosshair on screen with its
centre at (x%,y%). It is drawn with OVER -1 and uses the full screen.

**WARNING**

Do not use this!
