HIS\_USE$
=========

+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Syntax   |  HIS\_USE$                                                        |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Location |  History Device                                                   |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
