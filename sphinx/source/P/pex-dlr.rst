PEX$
====

+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Syntax   |  PEX$                                                             |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Location |  PEX                                                              |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+

This function returns the date of assembly, version and sub-version of
the PEX file.

**CROSS-REFERENCE**

:ref:`pex-save` alters the sub-version number.
See also :ref:`ql-pex` and
:ref:`pif-dlr`.
