PIE\_EX\_ON
===========

+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Syntax   |  PIE\_EX\_ON                                                      |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Location |  PIE                                                              |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+

This command re-enables PIE for SD.EXTOP system calls, so that they are
affected by the normal PIE\_ON and PIE\_OFF commands.

**CROSS-REFERENCE**

See :ref:`pie-on`.
