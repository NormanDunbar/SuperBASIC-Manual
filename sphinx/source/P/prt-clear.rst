PRT\_CLEAR
==========

+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Syntax   |  PRT\_CLEAR                                                       |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Location |  ST/QL, SMSQ/E                                                    |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+

This clears out all currently closed PRT buffers, thus preventing any
further output, in the same way as PAR\_CLEAR.

**CROSS-REFERENCE**

:ref:`par-clear` and
:ref:`ser-clear` are similar.
