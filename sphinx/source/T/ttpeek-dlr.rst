TTPEEK$
=======

+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Syntax   |  TTPEEK$ (adr, length)                                            |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Location |  QView Tiny Toolkit                                               |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+

See PEEK$.

**Example**

::

    PRINT TTPEEK$(TTV-2,2)

always shows the letters QV.
