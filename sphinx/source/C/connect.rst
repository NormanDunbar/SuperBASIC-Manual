CONNECT
=======

+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Syntax   |  CONNECT [#]pipe\_in% TO [#]pipe\_out%                            |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Location |  Turbo Toolkit                                                    |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+

This command is exactly the same as TCONNECT, except that the two
channels do not have to have a hash sign in front of them.
