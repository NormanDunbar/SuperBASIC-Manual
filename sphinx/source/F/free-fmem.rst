FREE\_FMEM
==========

+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Syntax   | FREE\_FMEM                                                        |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Location | SMSQ/E for Q68                                                    |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+

Returns the amount of free fast memory on a Q68.

**Note 1**

Should be about 10 kB on a freshly booted Q68
