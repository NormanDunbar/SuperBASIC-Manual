APPEND
======

+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Syntax   |  APPEND file1,file2                                               |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Location |  ST/QL                                                            |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+

This command allows you to merge two files together by appending file2
to the end of file1.

**NOTE**

Both file1 and file2 must include the device name.

**CROSS-REFERENCE**

The THOR XVI has a special form of :ref:`copy` which
is similar to this.
