JobCBS
======

+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Syntax   |  JobCBS ( {jobnr \| jobname} )                                    |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Location |  BTool                                                            |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+

This function is identical to JBASE, but the problems mentioned in the
notes above do not exist with JobCBS. Alas, it will not accept a jobnr
of -1 in current versions.
