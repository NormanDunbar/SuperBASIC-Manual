WDEL\_F
=======

+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Syntax   |  WDEL\_F [#ch,] [wild]                                            |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Location |  THORs                                                            |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+

WDEL\_F is exactly the same as WDEL except no prompts or information
about the files being deleted is shown on screen.
