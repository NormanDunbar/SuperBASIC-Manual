SUB
===

+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Syntax   |  ... SUB line                                                     |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Location |  QL ROM                                                           |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+

This keyword forms part of the SuperBASIC keyword GO SUB and has no
purpose on its own. Any attempt to use it on its own will cause a 'Bad
Line' error.
