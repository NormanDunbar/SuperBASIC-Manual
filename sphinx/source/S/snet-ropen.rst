SNET\_ROPEN
===========

+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Syntax   |  SNET\_ROPEN                                                      |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Location |  SMSQ/E, ATARI Emulators                                          |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+

This command reopens the serial ports for use by the SERNET
driver in case they have been closed by other programs.
