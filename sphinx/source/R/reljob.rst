..  _reljob:

RELJOB
======

+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Syntax   |  RELJOB jobId                                                     |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Location |  BTool                                                            |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+

Same as REL\_JOB apart from the fact that this expects the JobID of the
Job rather than its name or a simple job number.
