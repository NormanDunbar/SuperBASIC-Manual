ROM\_LOAD
=========

+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Syntax   |  ROM\_LOAD device\_file                                           |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Location |  ATARI\_REXT (pre v1.21 only)                                     |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+

On later versions of the Emulator, this has been renamed EPROM\_LOAD.
