BAT$
====

+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Syntax   |  BAT$                                                             |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Location |  Beuletools                                                       |
+----------+-------------------------------------------------------------------+

with the BAT\_USE command.
